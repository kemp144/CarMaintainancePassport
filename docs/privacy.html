Privacy Policy

Car Maintenance Passport

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Car Maintenance Passport is designed to be local-first and private by default. No account is required. Most data stays on your device, and the app does not use third-party ads or analytics.

What data the app stores

All data you enter — vehicles, service records, fuel logs, reminders, and documents — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

If iCloud backup is available, the app can save backup snapshots of your records to your personal iCloud Drive account. This is controlled entirely by your Apple ID and Apple's privacy policies. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No third-party analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting sent to third parties
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No account registration or login
  • No location data
  • No contacts or calendar access

In-app purchases

Car Maintenance Passport offers an optional Pro upgrade via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. The developer does not receive or store any payment information.

iCloud backup

The app saves automatic backup snapshots to iCloud Drive when iCloud storage is available for the installed build and enabled on your device. These backups are stored in your personal iCloud account under the "Car Maintenance Passport" folder. They are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

You can create a fresh backup snapshot or restore the latest backup from the app's Settings screen.

VIN lookup

If you tap the Autofill button after entering a VIN, the app sends that VIN to the U.S. National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) VIN decoder service to fetch vehicle make, model, and year data. This request happens only when you choose to use VIN autofill.

Document attachments

Photos and PDF documents you attach to service records are stored locally on your device. They are included in iCloud backups if backup is enabled. They are never uploaded to any external server.

Notifications

The app can send local notifications for service reminders. These are scheduled on-device by iOS and do not involve any external server or third-party service.

Children's privacy

Car Maintenance Passport does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts and no advertising or analytics SDKs. Optional VIN autofill uses the NHTSA VIN decoder only when triggered by the user.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in a material way, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please use the support link in the App Store listing or visit the support page.
